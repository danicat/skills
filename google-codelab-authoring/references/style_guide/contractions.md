  recommend using common two-word contractions such as you're, don't, and
  there's.\n\n\n## Negation contractions\n\nIn particular, we recommend using negation contractions such as isn't, don't, and
  can't. It's easy for a reader to miss the word not when they're scanning, whereas
  it's harder to misread don't as do.\n\nIf you need to emphasize the negative, you can use text formatting such as is
<em>not</em>, which renders as "is not." But in most cases, you don't
need emphasis to make your point clear.\n\n\n## Contractions to avoid\n\nDon't make up nonstandard contractions such as guides're or browser's (where
  's means is).\n\nDon't use three-word contractions such as mightn't've.\n
